This form can be used to submit confidential reports and information in accordance with Red Dots Compliance Reporting Policy (“Policy”). Your report will be communicated to the Audit Committee of Red Dot’s Board of Directors, who are charged with the oversight of investigations into matters reported under the Policy.

It is a violation of federal and state law, as well as of Red Dot’s policies governing employees, directors and independent contractors, to retaliate against any individual submitting or suspected of having submitted any such report. Any person who takes or threatens any adverse action against an employee on the basis of a known or suspected compliance report will be subjected to disciplinary action, which may include immediate termination for cause.
